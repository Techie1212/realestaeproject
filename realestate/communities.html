Landmark Ranch Estates

Landmark Ranch Estates, located within the lovely town of Southwest Ranches, is a luxurious gated community featuring palatial homes capturing the essence of South Florida refinement. Create your own custom home or select one of our model homes which span from 7,000 sq to over 20,000 sq: The Azuria, The Delano, The Biscayne and Calais Models. Premier Estate Collection.Homes sit on multi acres home sites and offer an abundance of privacy and space.

     

Privacy & comfort

Nestled in the heart of Southwest Ranches, Landmark Ranch Estates offers privacy along with proximity to the best attractions that the South Florida has to offer.

Located about 20 minutes away from Fort Lauderdale International Airport, enjoy access to a variety of dining and shopping opportunities nearby: Sawgrass Mills Mall, Bonefish Grill, Café 27, Pembroke Lakes Mall, Weston Town Center.

Golf enthusiasts, bask in the surrounding golf courses: Weston Hills Country Club, Bonaventure Country Club, Grand Palms Hotel, and Plantation Preserve Country Club. Families enjoy the numerous renowned private schools nearby along with Public schools such as Hawks Bluff Elementary, Falcon Cove Middle, and West Broward High.
